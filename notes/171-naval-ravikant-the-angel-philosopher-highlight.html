  • Naval’s Foundational Values (a few examples)
    Key takeaways:
    • Values are a set of things that one chooses not to compromise on and lives by.
    • Examples of foundational values include honesty, long-term thinking, pure relationships, and no anger.
    • Finding relationships, co-workers, and partners with aligned values is important for harmony.
    • Incompatible values often lead to disagreements and conflicts over trivial matters.

    Key takeaways:

    • Working on inner growth and self-improvement can be a lonely task.
